Terms of use

Last updated July 3, 2026

Use Eventully responsibly

Use Eventully to discover clubs, manage legitimate club listings, post accurate events, and communicate respectfully with members and officers.

Officer access

Only authorized club representatives should claim or manage a club. Admins may approve, reject, transfer, or remove officer access when needed to protect a listing or resolve a dispute.

Messages and content

Do not post harassment, spam, misleading event information, or content you do not have permission to share. Officers and admins can remove messages that do not belong on the platform.

University relationship

Eventully is not officially affiliated with the University of Washington. Official RSO status and records should be verified through university-managed systems.
